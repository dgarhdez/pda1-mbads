## How to Use

1. Click the "Open in Colab" badge for any notebook
2. The notebook will open in Google Colab
3. To save your work: **File > Save a copy in Drive**
4. Run cells with `Shift + Enter`

## Notes

- Colab has pandas, numpy, and plotly pre-installed
- Your changes are temporary unless you save a copy to your Drive
- Most notebooks are self-contained and create their own sample data
